01 How to Book Your Appointment

Booking with Glow Pavi's Makeover is simple and personal. We prefer to connect directly so we can understand exactly what you need for your special day.

💬
1
Reach Out
Contact us via WhatsApp, Instagram DM, or phone to discuss your requirements and check availability.
🗓️
2
Confirm Date
We'll confirm your date, discuss the service package, pricing, and any special requirements you have.
✦
3
Lock Your Slot
Pay the advance amount to officially secure your booking. Your date is reserved only after this step.

✦ Slots fill up quickly, especially for weekend and wedding season dates. We recommend booking at least 2–3 months in advance for bridal services.

02 Advance Payment

An advance payment is required to confirm and hold your appointment. The amount varies by service:

  • Bridal packages — 50% of the total package value
  • Party & event makeup — ₹500–₹1000 (as agreed at time of booking)
  • Skin care sessions — ₹300–₹500 (as agreed at time of booking)

The remaining balance is to be paid on or before the day of your appointment. We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and cash.

⚠️ Your appointment date is not confirmed until the advance payment is received. Do not assume your slot is held based on verbal communication alone.

03 Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both parties:

Notice Given Refund on Advance Status
7+ days before appointment Full refund of advance payment Full Refund
3–6 days before appointment 50% of advance refunded Partial Refund
24–72 hours before No refund — credit toward rebooking Credit Only
Less than 24 hours / Same day No refund, no credit No Refund
No-show Full advance forfeited Forfeited

To cancel your booking, please notify us via WhatsApp at your earliest convenience. We kindly ask that you do not wait until the last minute, as cancellations affect our scheduling and preparation.

04 Rescheduling

Life happens — and we are always happy to accommodate reasonable rescheduling requests.

  • Rescheduling requests made 72+ hours in advance are accommodated at no extra charge (subject to availability)
  • Rescheduling within 24–72 hours — we will do our best, but a rebooking fee of ₹200–₹500 may apply
  • Each booking may be rescheduled a maximum of once. A second rescheduling request will be treated as a new booking
  • Bridal bookings may be rescheduled up to 30 days before the original wedding date; after that, standard cancellation terms apply

🌸 We are always open to a conversation. If you are facing an emergency or genuinely difficult situation, please reach out and we will work with you as best we can.

05 Special Terms for Bridal Bookings

Bridal packages carry additional considerations given the significance and preparation involved:

  • Trial session — strongly recommended, must be scheduled 2–4 weeks before the wedding day
  • Date exclusivity — once your wedding date is confirmed with advance payment, we do not take other bridal bookings for that date
  • Travel bookings — if we travel to your venue, travel expenses (transportation, accommodation if overnight) will be discussed and agreed upon at booking
  • Early morning calls — for appointments before 6:00 AM, a small early-morning surcharge may apply
  • Additional family members — makeup for mothers, sisters, or bridesmaids must be booked and confirmed in advance

06 No-Shows

A no-show is when a client does not arrive for their appointment and does not provide any prior notice.

  • The full advance payment will be forfeited in all no-show cases
  • We will attempt to contact you once if you are more than 15 minutes late
  • After 30 minutes without contact, the slot will be considered a no-show
  • Clients with a history of no-shows may be required to pay the full service amount in advance for future bookings

07 If We Need to Cancel

We take our commitments to you extremely seriously. In the rare event that we need to cancel or reschedule:

  • We will notify you as early as possible via WhatsApp and/or phone
  • A full refund of your advance payment will be issued within 3–5 business days
  • We will do our best to find you an alternative date or recommend a trusted artist
  • For bridal cancellations by us, we will provide additional compensation as a goodwill gesture

✦ We have never cancelled a confirmed bridal booking. Your trust is something we protect with the utmost seriousness.
