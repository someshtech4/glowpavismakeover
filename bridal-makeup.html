Common Questions

Everything you
need to know

How far in advance should I book?
+
We recommend booking at least 3 to 6 months before your wedding, especially from October to February. Popular dates fill quickly, so booking early gives you the best choice of dates and packages.
Is a bridal trial included?
+
Yes. Every package includes a full trial session 2 to 4 weeks before your wedding. We use that time to refine your look together so your wedding morning runs smoothly and confidently.
Do you travel to the wedding venue?
+
Absolutely. We travel to homes and venues across Madurai and the surrounding areas. Travel charges are discussed clearly and transparently during your consultation so there are no surprises.
How long does bridal makeup take?
+
Most bridal sessions take 2.5 to 4 hours depending on the look and whether hair styling is included. We always schedule enough time so you never feel rushed on your most important day.
What products do you use?
+
We use premium professional brands known for longevity, HD performance, and skin safety. Every product we apply is dermatologically friendly and tested for long, comfortable wear across all skin types.
Can family members get makeup done too?
+
Of course. We offer add-ons for mothers, sisters, and bridesmaids. Simply let us know when you book so we can build the right schedule and make sure everyone feels beautiful on your big day.
