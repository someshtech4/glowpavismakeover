Common Questions

Everything you
need to know

How far in advance should I book?
+
We suggest booking at least 3 to 6 months before your wedding date, especially during peak season from October to February. Popular dates tend to fill up very quickly, so early booking gives you the best pick.
Is a bridal trial included?
+
Yes, every bridal package includes a trial session held 2 to 4 weeks before your wedding day. This gives us the chance to refine the look together so that on your wedding morning, everything runs smoothly.
Do you travel to the wedding venue?
+
Absolutely. We travel to homes and venues across Madurai and the surrounding areas. Travel charges are applied based on the distance and will be discussed clearly during your consultation.
How long does bridal makeup take?
+
It typically takes between 2.5 to 4 hours depending on the look and whether hair styling is part of the plan. We always schedule enough time so there is never any rushing on your big day.
What products do you use?
+
We use premium professional makeup brands known for their longevity, HD performance, and skin safety. All products we use are dermatologically friendly and tested for long wear throughout the day.
Can family members get makeup done too?
+
Of course. We offer add-on packages for mothers, sisters, and bridesmaids. Just let us know when you book so we can build the right schedule and make sure everyone looks their best.
