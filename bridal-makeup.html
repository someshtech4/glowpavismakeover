Common Questions

Everything you
need to know

How far in advance should I book?
+
We recommend booking at least 3–6 months before your wedding date, especially for peak season (October–February). Popular dates fill very quickly.
Is a bridal trial included?
+
Yes, all our bridal packages include a trial session 2–4 weeks before the wedding day. This ensures the final look is exactly as you envisioned.
Do you travel to the wedding venue?
+
Yes! We offer home visits and venue visits across Madurai and surrounding areas. Travel charges apply based on distance.
How long does bridal makeup take?
+
Typically 2.5–4 hours depending on the complexity of the look and whether hair styling is included. We always plan accordingly so you're never rushed.
What products do you use?
+
We use premium international and professional makeup brands known for longevity, skin safety, and HD performance. All products are dermatologically friendly.
Can family members also get makeup done?
+
Absolutely. We offer add-on packages for mothers, sisters, and bridesmaids. Please mention this during booking so we can plan the schedule.
