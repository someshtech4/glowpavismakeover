Privacy Policy

Your trust is sacred to us. Here is how we protect your personal information.

01 Information We Collect

When you book an appointment, contact us via WhatsApp, Instagram, or our website, or visit our studio, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Contact details — your name, phone number, email address, and location
  • Appointment details — your wedding date, event type, service preferences, and special requests
  • Skin & beauty information — skin type, allergies, sensitivities, or previous reactions you share with us for service personalisation
  • Photographs — images taken before, during, or after your makeup session (with your explicit consent)
  • Payment information — booking advance details (we do not store full payment card data)
  • Communication history — messages sent via WhatsApp, Instagram DM, or email

🌸 We collect only what is genuinely necessary to deliver a beautiful, personalised experience for you. We never ask for more than we need.

02 How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used exclusively to serve you better. Specifically:

  • To confirm, manage, and fulfil your bookings and appointments
  • To personalise your makeup and beauty services based on your skin type and preferences
  • To send appointment reminders, confirmations, and follow-up messages
  • To respond to your queries promptly via WhatsApp, Instagram, or phone
  • To share important updates such as schedule changes or policy updates
  • To improve our services based on feedback you voluntarily provide

We do not use your personal data for unsolicited marketing, third-party advertising, or automated profiling of any kind.

03 Information Sharing

We treat your personal information with the utmost confidentiality. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.

In limited circumstances, we may share information with:

  • Payment processors — only the information necessary to process your advance booking payment securely
  • Photographers or videographers — only your name and event date, if we collaborate for your bridal shoot, and only with your prior knowledge
  • Legal authorities — if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation

Any partner who receives your information is bound by confidentiality obligations and may not use it for any purpose beyond what is specified.

04 Photography & Image Policy

We take pride in showcasing our artistry. However, your consent is everything to us.

  • We will always ask your explicit verbal or written consent before photographing you for portfolio or social media use
  • If you consent, images may be shared on our Instagram, Facebook, or website gallery
  • You may request that your photos not be published at any time — before, during, or after your session
  • Watermarks or credits may be applied to published images
  • If you change your mind after publication, contact us and we will remove the images promptly

📸 No image will ever be posted without your knowledge. Your comfort and dignity come before any portfolio consideration.

05 Data Security

We implement reasonable and appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse:

  • Client records are stored securely and accessible only to authorised studio staff
  • WhatsApp and messaging communications are protected by end-to-end encryption
  • We do not store payment card numbers or sensitive financial data
  • Physical records (appointment books, consultation forms) are kept securely at our studio

No method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we use best practices, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

06 Cookies & Website

Our website may use basic cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our pages. This helps us improve our website experience.

  • Essential cookies — necessary for the website to function properly
  • Analytics — anonymous usage data to improve site performance (e.g. Google Analytics, if enabled)

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect certain website features.

07 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or outdated information
  • Right to deletion — request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal obligations
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw consent for photo use, marketing messages, or any other consent-based processing
  • Right to opt out — unsubscribe from any promotional communication at any time

To exercise any of these rights, simply contact us via WhatsApp or email and we will respond within 7 business days.

08 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Continued use of our services after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions about your privacy?

We are always here to help. If you have any concerns about how your data is handled, please reach out and we will be happy to clarify.
