Questions

Everything you
need to know

How long does party makeup take?
+
Usually 60–90 minutes for a natural look, and up to 2.5 hours for full glam with hair. We recommend booking a little early so you're never rushing before the event.
Can you come to my venue or home?
+
Yes! We offer home and venue visits across Madurai. Travel charges apply for locations beyond a certain distance. Just send us a message to check availability.
How long does the makeup last?
+
Party makeup is designed to hold strong for 8–12 hours using long-hold, sweat-proof products. So whether the party ends at 10pm or 2am — you're covered.
Do I need to bring anything?
+
Just yourself! We bring everything. One small tip: wear a front-open outfit during the session so it doesn't smudge your look when you change.
Can I request a specific look?
+
Absolutely. Share reference images from Pinterest or Instagram and we'll customise it to match your vision, outfit colour, and skin tone. The more you share, the better the result.
Do you do group bookings?
+
Yes — and we love them! Group bookings for family functions, bachelorette parties, and friend groups are welcome. Special rates apply for groups of 3 or more.
