Questions

Your skin care
questions answered

When should I start pre-bridal skin care?
+
Ideally 2–3 months before your wedding. This allows enough time for the full programme to work and for your skin to reach its peak glow on the big day.
Is it safe for sensitive skin?
+
Absolutely. We customise every treatment based on your skin's sensitivity level. We use only gentle, dermatologist-friendly products for reactive and sensitive skin types.
How many sessions do I need?
+
It depends on your skin's starting condition and your goals. The Bridal Glow Package is typically 4–6 sessions. A single facial or cleanup is a one-time session.
Can I combine skin care with makeup booking?
+
Yes — in fact, we strongly recommend it. Brides who complete the skin care programme before their bridal makeup session get noticeably better results. Combo packages available.
Will there be any side effects?
+
We avoid any aggressive chemical treatments that cause peeling or recovery time. All our treatments are designed to give visible results without downtime.
Do you offer home visit sessions?
+
Certain treatments are available as home visits within Madurai. Basic facials and cleanups can be done at your home. Please enquire for details and availability.
