FAQ

Common questions,
honest answers

Everything you need to know before booking your bridal experience.

We recommend booking 3 to 6 months in advance for bridal packages — especially for peak wedding season (November to February). For popular dates, bookings are taken up to a year in advance. WhatsApp us and we will check your date availability right away.

Yes — the Signature and Grand Bridal packages include a pre-bridal trial session. During the trial, we finalise your exact look so there are no surprises on your big day. Trials are usually conducted 1 to 4 weeks before the wedding.

Both options are available. We have a private studio in Madurai. We also offer home and venue visits within Madurai at no extra charge for the Grand package. For outstation travel outside Madurai, a travel fee applies — ask us for a quote.

A full bridal look typically takes 2 to 2.5 hours for the bride. If you have booked additional people, we factor in their time and always arrive early to ensure everyone is ready well before the ceremony.

We use premium internationally trusted brands including MAC, Huda Beauty, NYX, Kryolan, and professional airbrush products. For sensitive skin, we do a patch test during the trial and choose hypoallergenic alternatives where needed.

A small advance is taken to confirm your date. Cancellations made 30 or more days before the event receive a full refund. For cancellations within 30 days, the advance is non-refundable as the date is blocked exclusively for you. Rescheduling is flexible.

Absolutely. We have a full portfolio of 500 plus bride looks across different skin tones, styles, and ceremonies. Just WhatsApp us and we will share photos relevant to your look and occasion. You are also welcome to visit our studio for a no-obligation consultation.
