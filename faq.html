👰 Bridal Makeup
How far in advance should I book bridal makeup? +
We recommend booking your bridal makeup at least 2–4 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (October–January). Popular dates fill up very quickly. For the wedding trial, plan to have it 2–4 weeks before your wedding day so there's enough time for adjustments if needed.
Do you offer a bridal trial session? +
Yes — and we strongly recommend every bride books a trial session. The trial is an opportunity for us to understand your vision, test the look on your skin, and make adjustments before your big day. It also helps you feel confident and relaxed on your wedding morning. Trial sessions are booked separately and priced as discussed at booking.
Can you match my look to a reference photo? +
Reference photos are a wonderful starting point and we encourage you to bring them! We use your inspirations to understand your aesthetic — but we also tailor the look to what works beautifully for your unique features, skin tone, and personality. Our goal is not to copy a look, but to create one that makes you feel like the best version of yourself.
Do you travel to the venue for bridal makeup? +
Yes! We do travel to wedding venues in and around Madurai. For venues within the city, travel is included in most bridal packages. For venues outside Madurai, a travel fee will be discussed and agreed upon at the time of booking. We always ensure a stress-free, comfortable morning by arriving well before your scheduled start time.
Can you do makeup for the rest of the bridal party? +
Absolutely! We can accommodate makeup for the bride's mother, sisters, bridesmaids, and other family members. All additional members of the bridal party must be confirmed and included at the time of booking so we can plan the schedule properly. Walk-in additions on the wedding day cannot be guaranteed.
How long does bridal makeup take? +
A full bridal makeup session typically takes 2.5 to 3.5 hours, depending on the complexity of the look and whether hair styling is included. We always recommend starting earlier than you think necessary — a relaxed, unhurried morning creates the best results. We will discuss the timeline when planning your booking.
📅 Booking & Appointments
How do I book an appointment? +
The easiest way to book is via WhatsApp. Send us a message describing the service you need, your preferred date, and any details about your event. We'll check availability, discuss your requirements, and once the advance payment is received, your slot is confirmed. You can also reach us on Instagram DM.
Can I reschedule my appointment? +
Yes — life happens, and we understand. Rescheduling requests made at least 72 hours before your appointment can usually be accommodated at no extra charge, subject to availability. Requests within 72 hours may incur a small rebooking fee. Each booking can be rescheduled once. Please see our full Booking & Cancellation Policy for complete details.
What happens if I arrive late to my appointment? +
We ask all clients to arrive at least 10 minutes before their scheduled time. If you arrive late, we will do our best to accommodate you, but the session will still end at the originally scheduled time — so you may receive a shorter service. For significant delays (30+ minutes), we may need to reschedule. Please contact us via WhatsApp if you're running late.
Is my booking date guaranteed after the advance payment? +
Yes. Once your advance payment is received and confirmed by us, your date is exclusively reserved for you. We will not accept any conflicting bookings for that date. This is especially important for bridal bookings, where we commit your entire wedding morning to you and your family.
💄 Makeup & Products
What products and brands do you use? +
We use a curated mix of professional-grade international and Indian brands that are well-tested, long-lasting, and suitable for diverse Indian skin tones. Our kit includes products from brands known for their performance under camera, in humid weather, and across long wedding days. We are happy to share specific product details if you have particular concerns or allergies.
I have sensitive skin. Is it safe for me to use your services? +
Absolutely. We have worked with many clients with sensitive, reactive, or condition-prone skin. Please inform us in advance about any allergies, sensitivities, or skin conditions (eczema, rosacea, acne-prone skin, etc.). We will select products that are gentle and appropriate for your skin, and perform a small patch test if needed before your session.
How long will the makeup last? +
With our professional setting techniques and long-wear products, makeup typically lasts 10–14 hours. Longevity depends on your skin type (oily skin tends to break down makeup faster), the weather/humidity, and your activity level. We provide a few tips before you leave to help your look last as long as possible.
Do you do airbrush makeup? +
Yes! We are certified in airbrush makeup artistry. Airbrush application creates an exceptionally smooth, lightweight, and camera-ready finish that photographs beautifully. It is particularly popular for bridal looks. Ask about airbrush options when discussing your booking — it may be included in select packages or available as an add-on.
Can you do natural or minimal makeup looks? +
Absolutely — and we love creating them! Not every client wants full glam, and we respect that deeply. We are equally skilled at soft, dewy, skin-first looks that enhance your natural beauty while still being polished and long-lasting. Just share your preference during the consultation and we will tailor the look accordingly.
🌿 Skin Care & Pre-Bridal
What is a pre-bridal skin care programme? +
A pre-bridal skin care programme is a series of facials, treatments, and skin-prep sessions done in the weeks or months before your wedding. The goal is to address specific concerns (pigmentation, dullness, acne, uneven texture) and bring your skin to its healthiest, most radiant state — so makeup sits beautifully on your wedding day. We customise every programme to your skin type and timeline.
How early should I start pre-bridal skin care? +
Ideally, begin your pre-bridal programme 3–6 months before your wedding. This gives enough time for visible skin transformation. However, even a 4–6 week programme closer to the wedding can produce meaningful improvements. The earlier you start, the more dramatic and lasting the results. Contact us for a personalised consultation.
Is there downtime after skin care treatments? +
Most of our facial and skin care treatments have minimal to no downtime — you can resume your normal day immediately after. Some deeper treatments may cause mild redness or sensitivity for a few hours. We always advise on aftercare and will schedule treatments well in advance of important events so your skin has time to settle and glow.

Pricing varies depending on the services included, the complexity of the look, travel requirements, and whether hair styling is part of the package. What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and cash. For advance payments, we prefer UPI or bank transfer as it provides a clear record for both parties. Please ensure payments are made only to our official accounts — details will be shared during the booking process.
Is the trial session included in the bridal package price? +
This varies by package. Some of our bridal packages include a complimentary trial, while for others it may be offered at a discounted rate. We will clarify this clearly when discussing your package options. We always recommend a trial regardless — the confidence it gives you is invaluable.
Do you offer discounts for multiple services or bridal parties? +
Yes! When booking makeup for multiple members of the bridal party (bride + family), we offer bundled pricing that is more economical than individual bookings. The exact discount depends on the number of people and services required. Reach out to discuss your full requirements and we'll put together a comprehensive, value-packed quote.
