Questions

Everything you
need to know

How long does party makeup last and how long does the session take?
+
We use professional long-hold, sweat-proof products that keep your makeup fresh for 8–12 hours. Sessions typically take 60–90 minutes for a standard look, and up to 2.5 hours for full glam with hair styling.
Do you offer home visits across Madurai?
+
Yes! Home and venue visits are available across Madurai and nearby areas. Travel charges may apply beyond a certain distance. Just WhatsApp us and we'll work out the details with you easily.
Can I share reference looks from Instagram or Pinterest?
+
Absolutely — and we encourage it. Share your reference images and we'll customise the look to match your vision, outfit colour, and skin tone perfectly. No two looks are the same here.
Do you take group bookings for friends and family?
+
Yes — group bookings for bachelorette parties, friend groups, and family functions are very welcome. Special rates apply for groups of 3 or more. Get in touch and we'll put together a package for you.
