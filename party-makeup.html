Questions

Everything you
need to know

How long does party makeup take?
+
Usually 60–90 minutes for a standard look, and up to 2.5 hours for full glam with hair. We recommend booking early so you're never rushing.
Can you come to my venue or home?
+
Yes! Home and venue visits are available across Madurai. Travel charges apply for locations beyond a certain distance. Contact us for exact rates.
How long does the makeup last?
+
Party makeup is designed to last 8–12 hours with our long-hold, sweat-proof products. Great for events that go late into the night.
Do I need to bring anything?
+
Just yourself! We bring all professional tools and products. However, wearing a front-open outfit during the session prevents smudging when you change.
Can I request a specific look?
+
Absolutely. Share reference images from Pinterest or Instagram and we'll customise the look to match your vision, outfit colour, and skin tone.
Do you do group bookings?+
Yes — group bookings for family functions, bachelorette parties, and friend groups are available. Special rates apply for groups of 3 or more.
