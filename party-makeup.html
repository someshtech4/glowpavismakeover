Questions

Everything you
need to know

How long does party makeup take?
+
Usually 60–90 minutes for a standard look, and up to 2.5 hours for full glam with hair. We always recommend booking a little early so you're never rushing to the event.
Can you come to my venue or home?
+
Yes! Home and venue visits are available across Madurai. Travel charges apply for locations beyond a certain distance. Just reach out and we'll sort the details with you.
How long does the makeup last?
+
Party makeup is designed to hold strong for 8–12 hours using long-wear, sweat-proof products. Great for events that go well past midnight.
Do I need to bring anything?
+
Just yourself! We bring every professional tool and product needed. We do suggest wearing a front-open outfit during the session so nothing gets smudged when you change.
Can I request a specific look?
+
Absolutely — and we encourage it. Share your reference images from Pinterest or Instagram and we'll customise the look to match your vision, outfit colour, and skin tone perfectly.
Do you do group bookings?
+
Yes — group bookings for family functions, bachelorette parties, and friend groups are welcome. Special rates apply for groups of 3 or more. Get in touch and we'll work it out together.
